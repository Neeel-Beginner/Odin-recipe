Chicken Curry

It is delicious and flavorful. I like to pour the rich and creamy curry sauce over warm white rice before eating.



Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 lbs. chicken cut into serving pieces
  • 1 pack Knorr Ginataang Gulay Mix
  • 2 pieces red bell pepper sliced into squares
  • 2 pieces red onions chopped


Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pot. Pan fry the chicken pieces for 1 Â½ to 2 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the cooking pot. Set aside.
  2. Combine Knorr Ginataang Gulay recipe Mix with 2 cups water. Stir until well blended. Set aside.
  3. Heat remaining oil on the pot. Saute onion and garlic. Add the celery. Continue to cook until onion and celery softens.
  4. Put the daikon radish in. Cook for 3 to 5 minutes.
